Fence Repair in Orlando, FL

Fast, reliable repairs for storm damage, rotting posts, leaning panels, and broken gates across Greater Orlando.

Expert Fence Repair You Can Count On

Florida weather can be tough on fences. Whether a storm knocked down a section, your posts are rotting, or your gate won't close properly, our team provides fast, professional repairs to restore your fence to like-new condition.

Common Repairs We Handle

  • Storm & wind damage — Fallen panels, broken posts, and structural damage from hurricanes and severe storms.
  • Rotting & decaying wood — Post replacement, board swaps, and re-staining to extend your fence's life.
  • Leaning or sagging fences — Post resetting, bracing, and realignment to straighten your fence line.
  • Broken or stuck gates — Hinge replacement, latch repair, and hardware upgrades for smooth operation.
  • Rust & corrosion — Chain link and aluminum panel repair, re-coating, and section replacement.

Our Repair Process

  1. Free inspection — We assess the damage, identify the root cause, and provide an honest evaluation.
  2. Written estimate — A clear, itemized quote with no surprises or hidden charges.
  3. Fast scheduling — Most repairs are completed within days, not weeks.
  4. Quality repair — We match existing materials and colors for a seamless, lasting fix.

When to Repair vs. Replace

Not sure if your fence needs repair or full replacement? We'll give you an honest recommendation. In general, if more than 50% of the fence is damaged or the posts are severely compromised, a full replacement may be more cost-effective. For localized damage, a targeted repair saves you money while restoring full function.
